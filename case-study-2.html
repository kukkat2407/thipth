Case Study · Real Estate · Batdongsan.com.vn

Increase user trust on the marketplace

Overview

Project Verified Listing Improvement
Users Consumer
My role Product Designer - owned end-to-end design
Stakeholders Head of Product, Product Manager, Researcher, Data Analyst, Engineering Manager, Developer, QA
Timeline Sep – Oct 2025
Impact Verified listing pageview share increased from 4.8% (Sep 2025) to ~9.3% (Dec 2025)

Problem statement

Fake listings and incorrect pricing is the #1 complaint consistently raised by consumers through multiple channels - hotline support, user interviews, surveys, and more.

  • Listing quality complaints account for ~20% of total user feedback (General feedback survey, Jan 2024 – Aug 2025, n = 1,089)
  • Average reported listings per month = ~1,000 (0.4% of total daily active listings)

Verified Listing (Tin xác thực) aims to raise the quality bar for listings on Batdongsan.com.vn by creating a dynamic that rewards real and accurate listings - increasing consumer satisfaction and trust with the platform.

After we improved the verification process on the supplier side (sellers), the main metrics dropped significantly from Aug – Sep 2025. On the demand side (consumers), feature awareness was very low - many consumers had never heard of this feature.

We hypothesised that improving consumer awareness and adoption would increase the number of Verified listings, Verified listing pageviews, and leads per Verified listing on the platform.

Design solution

Redesign the label - new branding

Since user awareness was very low, I stepped outside the existing design system to create a new label and branding that represents one of Batdongsan.com.vn's most important features.

New UI to promote Verified listings on search results

The new UI shows a clear, concise explanation of what a Verified listing is. It's also shorter and more scannable on small screen devices.

Consistent explanation across all platforms and screens

Rewrote the explanation banner on the Verified listing detail page for consistency and clarity across web and mobile.

Drive adoption - suggest more Verified listings

Re-ordered the Verified Listing filter position on the filter bar and revealed the explanation content instead of hiding it behind an info icon.

Impact

  1. Verified listing pageview share hit ~9.3% in Dec 2025 - up from 4.8% in Sep 2025
  2. Only 0.2% of Verified listings were reported by users (Nov 2025) - demonstrating very high listing quality and earned consumer trust
  3. The Marketing team adopted the new designs in multiple ad campaigns and banners
